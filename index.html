CYBERPUNK GROUND DEFENCE

LOCAL CO-OP SHOOTER


About

Cyberpunk ground defence is a web based 2D local co-op shoot-em-up. You and a friend take control of the cities defence cannons to guard against the incoming invasion.

Gain points by eliminating the enemies, and use these points to gain health for you or your partner, gain lives, use special projectiles, or save them to win the game.
