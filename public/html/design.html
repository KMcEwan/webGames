Game overview

Cyberpunk ground defence is a local co-op shoot-em-up game in which each player will control a cannon of which there will be two of within the scene. These cannons are used to protect the city from enemies. Score is gained by destoying the enemies, which can then be used to purchase special abilites and win the game.


Special abilities

Points are used to win the game, as well as purchasing special abilities. Each player will be able to spend their points on gaining back full health for themselves or themselves and their partner. Player 1 will be able to spend points on gaining a life back for each player, and player 2 will be able to use theirs on a special laser ability.


Game progression

Game progression is based on the points that the players gain from destroying the enemies, the greater the number of points the players have the more difficult the game will become as the enemy spawn rate will increase. As the players have their own scoring system, the points used to decide the game progression, is based on the player with the most points.


Art Style

The visual design for this game is Cyberpunk with an emphasis on neon blues and pinks. The cyberpunk style focuses on futuristic worlds that feature advances in science and technology within a dystopian setting.


Inspriation

The inspiration for the gameplay for this game came from two classics, Space Invaders and Asteriods. The use of the cannons within the game that the player will control was taken from the milsile ship from Space invaders, and the randomness of the enemies was taken from Asteroids. The art style of the game was taken from the game Cloudpunk.


Game design

The entire game will be played within the one scene. Within this scene, the players will be displayed their health, lives and their score. Enemies will spawn from the top of the screen and move downwards to the city which will line the bottom of the screen. The players will be able to move left and right, and will be placed above the city.
