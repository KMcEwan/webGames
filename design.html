Game overview

Cyberpunk ground defence is a local co-op shoot-em-up game in which each player will control a cannon of which there will be two of within the scene. These cannons are used to protect the city from enemies. Score is gained by destoying the enemies, which can then be used to purchase special abilites and win the game.


Special abilities

Points are used to win the game, as well as purchasing special abilities. Each player will be able to spend their points on gaining back full health for themselves or, themselves and their partner, gain a life back for themself, or the ability to fire a special type of projectile.


Game progression

Game progression is based on points, the greater the points the player has, the more difficult the game becomes as the enemy spawn rate increases.
